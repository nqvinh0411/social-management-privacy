Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Social Management" application ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our services to manage your Facebook Pages, Instagram, and Threads accounts.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your Facebook, Instagram, or Threads account to our app, we may access and process:

  • Public profile information (name, profile picture)
  • Page and Instagram account information you manage
  • Content you authorize us to publish (posts, images, videos)
  • Basic engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares) related to published content

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Publish and manage content on your behalf, as authorized by you
  • Provide scheduling and content management features
  • Display basic performance metrics of published content

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and content data are stored securely and are only accessible by the account owner. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 5).

5. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below. We will remove your data from our systems within 30 days of a verified request.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with Meta Platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Threads) APIs. Your use of these platforms is also subject to Meta's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
